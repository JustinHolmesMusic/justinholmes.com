Justin Myles Holmes

VOWEL SOUNDS

Vowel Sounds is the debut album-length offering from Justin Myles Holmes.

Recorded in Nashville with a hand-picked ensemble of award-winning bluegrass session musicians, it packs a hard-driving acoustic feel along with the wind instruments and varied singing techniques that have made Holmes' live shows a draw at blockchain events around the world.

The album will be released on the Ethereum blockchain, with the record itself encrypted and available for download once the contract reaches 10ETH.

What is this?

This is a new kind of music release. It's a way to fund the creation of a record, and to distribute it in a way that is fair to the artist and the audience. It's a way to make sure that the record is available to everyone, and that the artist is compensated for their work.

So how does it work?

The record is encrypted and available for download. Once contributions have crossed the threshold of 10ETH, anyone can download the encrypted record. The decryption key will be made available on the Ethereum blockchain using Threshold Network's Access Control (TACo). Once released, the record will be in the public domain, free to download and use under a creative commons license.
